I am a Masters Student studying Artificial Intelligence and doing human-centered computing research to try and figure out how we can design technology that helps people. Healthcare is a particularly rich area for this type of work, and I have been very lucky to learn this first hand by working on interesting problems with interesting people in the Personal Data and Empowerment Lab under [Professor Jason Wiese](https://pedel.cs.utah.edu/) and for the Department of Biomedical Informatics under the supervision of [Dr. Jorie Butler](https://joriebutlerlab.research.st/). 

I'm growing increasingly interested in and trying to explore how to build AI systems that help people accomplish tasks and answer questions in a way that empowers them to do what they think is right, how they think it should be done.

This project is an interactive exploration of U.S. life expectancy and mortality data over the last century, developed using **D3.js**. It correlates public health trends with major technological and medical innovations.

**Key Features:**
* **Linked Visualizations:** A primary line graph tracks life expectancy, synchronized with a brushable timeline and secondary bar charts for cause-of-death breakdowns.
* **Interactive Discovery:** Clicking the main graph populates a research panel with relevant technological innovations and Wikipedia resources for the selected era.
* **Comparative Analysis:** Users can generate static "snapshots" of the main visualization to compare different historical timeframes side-by-side.
* **Dynamic Filtering:** A left-hand control panel allows for standalone or comparative data overlays, updated in real-time across all views.

Self-Supervised vs Supervised Vision Models

Conducted a comparative analysis between I-JEPA (a self-supervised vision model) and Google's Vision Transformer for image classification on CIFAR-10. The study explores the trade-offs between classification accuracy and semantic understanding in self-supervised vs supervised learning approaches.
